Advanced AI at its Core

Flowframes leverages multiple cutting-edge AI models, allowing you to choose the best one for your content. The primary model, RIFE (Real-Time Intermediate Flow Estimation), provides an exceptional balance of speed and quality.

  • RIFE: The default choice for outstanding, real-time performance, especially on Nvidia GPUs.
  • DAIN: An alternative model known for its high-quality output.
  • Experimental Models: Access to newer models like FLAVR and XVFI for users who want to push the boundaries (Patreon exclusive).

Maximum Reach & Flexibility

We believe powerful tools should be accessible. Flowframes is designed to work with a vast range of hardware and file types.

  • GPU Support: Full support for Nvidia (CUDA), AMD (Vulkan), and Intel (Vulkan) GPUs.
  • Input Formats: Works with MP4, GIF, WEBM, MKV, MOV, and even image sequences (PNG, JPG).
  • Output Formats: Export to modern codecs like H265/HEVC, VP9, and AV1 to maintain quality while saving space.
  • Audio & Subtitles: All original audio and subtitle tracks are perfectly preserved and synced in the final output. No quality loss.

Intelligent Processing for Flawless Results

Flowframes thinks about your video to prevent common issues and preserve artistic intent, delivering a polished final product automatically.

  • Scene Detection: Automatically identifies cuts between scenes to prevent ugly artifacts and ghosting.
  • Frame De-Duplication: Smartly ignores repeated frames (common in animations) to avoid unnatural motion.
  • Speed Compensation: A special mode for 2D animation that maintains the original pacing and timing.
